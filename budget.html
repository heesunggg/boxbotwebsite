Budget Commentary and Allocation Rationale

Our team prioritized spending on high-quality drive motors and the main microcontroller to ensure precision and reliability, which were key performance metrics. We actively chose to use readily available materials (like scrap wood and lab fasteners) for the chassis construction to keep the total cost well below the $250 limit, retaining a healthy buffer for unexpected purchases.
