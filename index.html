Project Summary

BoxBot is an innovative autonomous robotics project developed over the course of a semester. Our objective was to create a fully autonomous, non-tethered robot capable of identifying cardboard boxes, aligning with them precisely, lifting them safely, and transporting them to a designated landing zone without human intervention.

The project integrates multiple engineering disciplines including mechanical design, electrical systems, embedded firmware, and autonomous control algorithms. Through iterative design and testing phases, our team successfully developed a system that demonstrates the practical applications of autonomous robotics in material handling tasks.
