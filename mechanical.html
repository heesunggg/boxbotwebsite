Mechanical Design & CAD

This is the CAD of BoxBot. We decided that we wanted the drivebase to be as low to the ground as possible, squeeze the box with with a rack and pinion system, and lift up the box with a scissor lift mechanism.

Drive Base

The foundation of BoxBot is a holonomic drive system designed for high maneuverability in constrained environments.

  • **Omni-Directional Movement**: Utilizes four 72mm Omni Wheels to allow for lateral and diagonal alignment.
  • **Stability**: Includes Roller Ball Transfer Bearings at key balance points to prevent tipping during heavy lifts.
  • **Material**: Constructed from 1/4" 6061 Aluminum for a rigid, low-flex platform.

Center Plate & Power Housing

The center plate serves as the structural bridge and the primary housing for heavy electronics and power cells.

  • **Weight Distribution**: Houses the 24V 4Ah battery centrally to optimize the center of gravity.
  • **Electronics Protection**: Provides a secure mounting surface for the Raspberry Pi 2 and Sabertooth motor driver.
  • **Circuit Safety**: Integrates a 6-Circuit LED Blade Fuse Block for immediate visual diagnostics and protection.

Scissor Lift Mechanism

A symmetrical dual-scissor system provides the vertical displacement needed to handle standard cardboard boxes.

  • **High Torque Actuation**: Driven by 25kg digital servos to ensure reliable lifting under load.
  • **Precision Travel**: Employs linear ball bearings and 8mm stainless steel rods to eliminate binding.
  • **Lateral Adjustment**: Integrated with a 32-pitch rack and pinion system to adjust for various box widths.
