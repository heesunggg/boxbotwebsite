Group formation, timeline managements, and honing down on a singular idea.

Group Formation & Roles

Our team came together because of our interest in creating a strong project to put on our personal websites. When discussing project teaming and agreement, we came up with these following rules:

  • Commitment: Spend 5-7 hours outside of class on a typical week, 30-40 hours in the last week.
  • Expectations: Call people out if someone is slacking off, and if it gets worse, involve the teaching team.
  • Project Manager: We decided a Project manager system, where Heesung will manage timelines and making sure work gets done.
Timeline Management

To ensure that we stayed on track, we used a Gantt chart to visualize the project timeline and assign clear, descriptive tasks to each team member. This improved accountability and helped us better understand how limited our time was for completing the project.

Figure 1: Sprint 1 Schedule- Gantt Chart

Honing a Singular Idea

We had many ideas, such as a Red Light Green Light Robot, CNC Tube Notcher, wall climbing robot, and more. Using a Pugh Matrix, we ended up with BoxBot. At this current stage, we didn't know exactly how we wanted to manipulate the box.

Figure 2: Pugh Matrix Chart

Figuring out the weeds

After settling on what we wanted the project to do, we spent several days figuring out what mechanism we wanted on the BoxBot. After several hours, we ended up with a U shaped robot, where the sides will be moved by a rack and pinion system while the box will be lifted up through a mechanism.

Phase 3: System Integration

MECHANICAL

+

Assembling primary load-bearing and lift systems.

ELECTRICAL

+

Integrating high-power components and safety.

SOFTWARE

+

Autonomous navigation and sensor fusion logic.

  • Installed 8mm stainless steel rods and linear ball bearings.
  • Assembled 32-pitch rack and pinion for width adjustment.
  • Mounted 72mm Omni Wheels for holonomic movement.
  • Wired the 24V battery through the Sabertooth 2x12 driver.
  • Integrated a 6-circuit LED blade fuse block for safety.
  • Fused RealSense depth data for obstacle avoidance.
  • Implemented state-machine for "detect, approach, squeeze".
